About me...

My desire to solve real world problems with the aide of technology, led me to enroll at Georgia Perimeter College in 2012. During a semester, while taking Intro to Computer Science and Human Devolpement simultaneuosly, I began noticing similarities between how humans develope and how computer systems operate. For instance, both humans and computer systems require a need for input and data. We process data with the aid of our senses while computers process data by means of functions and methods. Due to unforseen circumstances I was unable to further my college education. However, I began educating myslef by learning Object Oriented Programming in JavaScript. In about a year I was able build several static and dynamic pages which demonstates my understanding of OOP, HTML5, CSS3, Node.js and so much more.
